Interesting Facts About Keyboards

  • The QWERTY layout was designed in 1868 for typewriters to prevent key jams.
  • Mechanical keyboards use individual switches under each key for better tactile feedback.
  • Some keyboards use optical or Hall effect switches for faster response times.
  • Ergonomic keyboards are designed to reduce strain and prevent repetitive stress injuries.
  • Gaming keyboards often feature RGB lighting and programmable macro keys.
  • Wireless keyboards can use various technologies like Bluetooth or proprietary RF.
  • Some keyboards are designed to be water-resistant or even fully waterproof.
