Interesting Facts About Motherboards

  • The first motherboards were introduced in the late 1970s.
  • ATX is the most common motherboard form factor today.
  • Some high-end motherboards have built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth capabilities.
  • The BIOS or UEFI firmware is stored on the motherboard.
  • Modern motherboards can support multiple GPUs for enhanced graphics performance.
  • Some motherboards have LED lighting for aesthetic purposes.
  • The chipset on a motherboard determines many of its features and capabilities.
